Yale: Financial Markets

Pre-College / Semester 1

Foundational knowledge and a strong early signal on your LinkedIn profile that you are serious about finance before college even starts.

CFI: FMVA (Select Modules)

Year 1 & 2

Don't pay for the whole thing unless you want to. Use the free courses for Accounting Fundamentals, Excel Crash Course, and basic 3-Statement modeling.

CFA Level 1

Summer after Year 2

The gold standard for Indian non-target students trying to break into finance. Shows immense quantitative rigor and commitment. Requires ~300 hours.

⚠ Critical OS Warnings

  • Do not sacrifice GPA for Certifications. A 9.0+ SGPA with zero certs is better than an 8.0 SGPA with CFA Level 1.
  • Do not apply through portals. Non-target resumes die in ATS systems. You must use cold emails and your Big 4 referrals.
  • Avoid "Trading" as a talking point. Investment banking interviews are about corporate finance and valuation, not day-trading or crypto.
